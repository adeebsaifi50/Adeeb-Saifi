About: Rishikesh is a peaceful town along the Ganges, known for yoga and adventure.

Haridwar

About: Haridwar is one of the oldest cities and a major pilgrimage site in India.

My Experience: Watching the evening Aarti at Har Ki Pauri was spiritual and powerful.

Mussoorie

About: Mussoorie is a beautiful hill station in Uttarakhand known as the Queen of the Hills.

My Experience: I enjoyed walking on Mall Road and the mountain views were amazing.

About: Mathura is the birthplace of Lord Krishna and a major spiritual city.

My Experience: Temples and street life were full of energy and devotion.

My Experience: The chants, temples and atmosphere made it feel truly divine.
