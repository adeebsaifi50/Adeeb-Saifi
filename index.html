My Favorite Travel Destinations

Rishikesh

The Yoga Capital of the World, known for its spiritual vibes, adventure sports, and the iconic Lakshman Jhula.

Haridwar

A sacred city where the Ganges River leaves the mountains, famous for Har Ki Pauri and the evening Ganga Aarti.

Mussoorie

The Queen of the Hills, a popular hill station offering panoramic views of the Himalayas and lush green valleys.

Vrindavan (Prem Mandir)

A holy town associated with Lord Krishna's childhood, home to numerous temples including the magnificent Prem Mandir.
